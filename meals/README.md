# The 30 Meals

Our core rotation. These are automatic, repeatable, and scalable.

## Breakfasts (4)

| # | Meal | Variations | Notes |
|---|------|------------|-------|
| B1 | Oats | Steel-cut, rolled, overnight | Weekday default |
| B2 | Whole grain toast | Peanut butter, avocado, eggs | Quick backup |
| B3 | Pancakes | Whole wheat, oat, or buckwheat | Weekend treat |
| B4 | Smoothie bowl | Fruit + oats + nut butter | Summer option |

## Lunches (4)

| # | Meal | Variations | Notes |
|---|------|------------|-------|
| L1 | Leftovers | Dinner becomes lunch | Default, no extra cooking |
| L2 | Soup + bread | Bean, lentil, vegetable | Big batches, freezer friendly |
| L3 | Grain bowl | Rice, quinoa, or farro base | Use leftover veg |
| L4 | Simple sandwich | Whole grain, hummus, vegetables | Emergency option |

→ [Lunch recipes](lunches/)

## Dinners (22 slots, 6-8 cores)

Our dinner rotation centers on **6-8 core meals** with seasonal variations.

### Core Dinners

| # | Meal | Base | Variations |
|---|------|------|------------|
| D1 | Lentil/Bean Soup | Legumes, vegetables, broth | Red lentil, minestrone, black bean |
| D2 | Curry | Dal, chickpea, or vegetable | Indian, Thai, or simple spiced |
| D3 | Chili | Beans, tomatoes, spices | With corn, over baked potato |
| D4 | Pasta | Whole wheat, legume-based | Marinara, aglio e olio, white bean |
| D5 | Stir-fry | Tofu/vegetables, rice | Different vegetables, sauces |
| D6 | Burrito Bowls | Beans, rice, salsa, guac | Taco bar format for guests |
| D7 | Mediterranean Plate | Hummus, falafel, salads, pita | Mezze spread for guests |
| D8 | Simple Dal + Rice | Red lentils, spices, rice | Emergency, 30-minute meal |

### Variation Strategy

Same core, different details:

- **Grain:** Rice → Quinoa → Couscous → Bulgur
- **Vegetables:** Seasonal rotation (roasted, steamed, raw)
- **Spices:** Keep it mild for sensitive palates, add heat at the table
- **Toppings:** Herbs, lemon, hot sauce, nutritional yeast

## Rules for Adding Meals

1. Must feed 6 people without stress
2. Must use ingredients on our standard shopping list
3. Must be acceptable to all 4 kids (or easily modified)
4. Must work as leftovers for lunch
5. No more than 30 total
