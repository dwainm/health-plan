# Contributing to Health Plan

## Adding a New Meal

1. **Test it first** — Make it at least 3 times
2. **Check the count** — Are we at 30 yet? If yes, something must go
3. **Follow the format** — See existing recipes for structure
4. **Update the index** — Check off in `meals/README.md`

## Recipe Format

```markdown
# Recipe Name

Brief description.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount | Notes |
|------------|--------|-------|
| ... | ... | ... |

## Method

1. Step one
2. Step two

## Family Notes

- What everyone thinks
- Variations that work
- Scaling for guests

---

*Prep time: X min | Cook time: Y min | Serves: 6*
```

## Principles Over Recipes

- Simple > impressive
- Repeatable > novel
- Whole food > processed
- Satiety > calorie counting

## No-Go List

- Recipes requiring special equipment
- Ingredients not at standard grocery store
- Separate meals for kids and adults
- More than 30 daily meals or 10 guest meals

---

*When in doubt, simplify.*
