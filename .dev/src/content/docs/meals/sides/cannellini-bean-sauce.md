title: Cannellini White Bean Sauce
---
![Cannellini Bean Sauce](/images/recipes/cannellini-bean-sauce.jpg)

**Plant-based | 3 ingredients | 5 minutes | Versatile**

A simple, creamy sauce that works as a dressing, dip, spread, or topping. Just three ingredients blended into a smooth, protein-rich sauce. Serve over kale, greens, salads, grains, veggies, or use as a sandwich spread or pizza topping.

---

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount | Notes |
|------------|--------|-------|
| Cannellini beans | 1 can (15 oz) | No-salt-added, drained and rinsed |
| Garlic | 1-2 cloves | Adjust to taste |
| Low-sodium tamari | 2 tbsp | Or soy sauce |
| Water | As needed | For consistency |

---

## Method

1. **Blend**: Purée beans, garlic, and tamari in food processor until very smooth.

2. **Adjust consistency**: Add water as needed—more water for a thinner dressing, less for a thicker dip.

---

## Tips

**Flavor boosters**: Add lemon juice, paprika, cumin, or fresh herbs

**Creamier**: Add a tablespoon of tahini or cashews

**Spicy**: Blend in red pepper flakes or jalapeño

---

## Variations

**Caesar-style**: Add nutritional yeast, Dijon mustard, and lemon juice

**Herb**: Blend in fresh basil, parsley, or dill

**Roasted garlic**: Use roasted garlic for milder, sweeter flavor

---

## Storage

- **Refrigerate**: Up to 5 days in airtight container
- **Tip**: Sauce thickens as it sits—add water to thin if needed

---

## Nutrition (per 2 Tbsp, 12 servings)

- **Calories**: ~33
- **Protein**: 2g
- **Carbs**: 6g
- **Fiber**: 1g
- **Fat**: 0g
